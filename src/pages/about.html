About the University of Lahore

Empowering generations through innovation, research, and world-class education since 1999.

Our Story

Founded in 1999, the University of Lahore (UOL) has grown into one of Pakistan’s largest private sector universities, renowned for its excellence in education, research, and community service.

With campuses across major cities of Pakistan, UOL offers a wide range of programs in Engineering, Medicine, Law, Sciences, Business, and Arts, fostering an environment of academic rigor and creativity.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To deliver quality education and promote research that contributes to the economic and social development of Pakistan and beyond.

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution known for excellence, innovation, and leadership in education, research, and community impact.
